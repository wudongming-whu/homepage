About Me

 I'm a second-year Ph.D. student in Intelligent Data Extraction, Analysis and Applications of Remote Sensing (RSIDEA) Group. My research interest include computer vision, high resolution remote sensing image understanding and intelligent interpretation and application of medical images.

  • 2019.9-Now           Ph.D in LIESMARS, Wuhan University. Supervisor: Prof. Yanfei Zhong & Liangpei Zhang
  • 2017.9-2019.6       M.E. in LIESMARS, Wuhan University. Supervisor: Prof. Yanfei Zhong & Liangpei Zhang
  • 2013.9-2017.6       B.E. in School of GeoSciences and Info-Physics, Central South University.
